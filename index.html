What We Do

Our Key
Initiatives

Four focused areas of sustainable impact — each designed to create lasting positive change in communities that need it most.

01
🎓
Education

Education
Empowerment

Adopting government schools across rural Karnataka — upgrading infrastructure, training teachers, and ensuring every student has access to quality, inclusive education.

  • Classroom upgrades, libraries & science labs
  • Teacher training in modern pedagogy & technology
  • Scholarships for economically disadvantaged students
  • Parental engagement & community programs
02
🏥
Healthcare

Community
Healthcare

Bringing quality healthcare to underserved communities through free health camps, preventive care, and sustainable healthcare infrastructure in rural areas.

  • Free general health, dental & eye screening
  • Women's health & maternal care programs
  • Nutrition, disease prevention & wellness sessions
  • Essential medicines & specialist referrals
03
📱
Flagship

Dhrustikona —
Digital Awareness

Our pioneering initiative addressing mobile phone addiction (Nomophobia) — guiding 50,000+ students, parents and educators toward digital balance through expert-led sessions with IPS officers, psychiatrists & doctors.

Program Impact

A little internet use is positive; excessive use harms mental wellbeing. We encourage participants to unplug, reconnect with nature and build real relationships. Let's save a generation together.

04
👩
Empowerment

Women's
Empowerment

Built on Dr. Shilpa's doctoral research on economic empowerment through sericulture — creating sustainable livelihoods and leadership pathways for women in rural Karnataka.

  • Sericulture training & traditional craft development
  • Market linkage & entrepreneurship support
  • Leadership development & confidence workshops
  • Microfinance guidance & SHG formation

Our Founder

Dr. Shilpa
H. Naveen

Founder & Chairperson · Educator · Social Entrepreneur

With a Ph.D. in Economics from KSOU Mysore and 17+ years as Professor, HOD and College Coordinator — Dr. Shilpa's journey from humble beginnings to national recognition is a story of resilience, purpose and transformative leadership.

She is also Co-Founder of Vaibhav Veda Green Ventures and a highly sought-after speaker across India — inspiring educators, entrepreneurs and change-makers through her visionary work.

"Aspiration is the main fuel for progress."
